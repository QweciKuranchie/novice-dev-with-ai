The 5-Part Prompt Formula

To get elite code out of AI models, don't chat. Architect your instructions.

Part 1

Role

Tell the AI who it is. Sets the persona and skill level.

"Act as an accessibility advocate..."
Part 2

Context

State the background details of your environment and tech stack.

"I'm a student writing vanilla CSS..."
Part 3

Task

Describe exactly what you need built or explained.

"Create a 3-card grid layout..."
Part 4

Constraints

Establish strict limits to prevent bloated code or frameworks.

"No inline styles. No React. Under 30 lines."
Part 5

Format

Define the layout of the AI's final answer block.

"Output HTML first, then CSS..."

The Power of Constraints

Without Constraints ("Write a button")

<div class="btn" style="color: blue; padding: 10px;" onclick="location.href='#'">
  <span>Click Me!</span>
</div>
<!-- Issues: Non-semantic div, inline styling, JS redirect, poor accessibility -->

With Constraints (Semantic & Accessible)

<button type="button" class="btn-primary">
  Submit Form
</button>
<!-- Clean, semantic, fully keyboard-accessible, styled cleanly in CSS -->
